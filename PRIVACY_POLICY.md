# LangCI Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 10, 2026
**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

LangCI ("we", "us", "the app") is an auditory-verbal therapy training app designed for people with cochlear implants and their families. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## The short version

LangCI is a **local-only app**. Everything you do in LangCI — family member profiles, voice recordings, drill scores, practice history — is stored only on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your data. We do not have accounts. We do not track you. We do not sell or share anything.

## What LangCI stores on your device

When you use LangCI, the following information is saved inside the app's private storage on your iPhone or iPad:

- **Family member profiles** you create (name, relationship, preferred language/dialect, optional baseline frequency setting, avatar color)
- **Voice recordings** you make during "familiar voice" setup and reading-aloud drills
- **Drill session history** (which drills you did, your scores, timestamps)
- **App preferences** (selected theme, reminder times, accessibility options)

All of this data lives inside your app sandbox and is backed up only if you have iCloud Backup enabled for your device in iOS Settings. LangCI itself never uploads this information anywhere.

## What LangCI does NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal account information.
- We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
- We do not track your location.
- We do not read your contacts, photos, calendar, or health data.
- We do not fingerprint your device or share identifiers with any third party.

## Microphone access

LangCI requests microphone access so you can record voice samples during training drills. Recordings are saved only to your device's private app storage and are used solely inside the app. You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings → LangCI → Microphone.

## Notifications

LangCI may ask to send you local notifications (practice reminders). These notifications are scheduled on your device by iOS; LangCI does not use push notifications and does not send any data to a server to deliver them.

## Children

LangCI is designed for use by people with cochlear implants, which can include children and their caregivers. Because LangCI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information off of the device, it does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents and guardians manage family member profiles directly on the device.

## Data retention and deletion

Because LangCI stores data only on your device, you are in full control:
- To delete an individual family member, drill result, or recording, use the in-app delete options.
- To delete all LangCI data at once, delete the app from your device. This will permanently remove the app's sandbox, including the database and all recordings.

## Medical disclaimer

LangCI is a **general-purpose auditory training aid**, not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent hearing loss, cochlear implant malfunction, or any other condition. Always consult a qualified audiologist, ENT specialist, or auditory-verbal therapist for medical advice, hearing assessments, device programming, and therapy decisions.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change how LangCI handles data, we will update this page and update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will also be highlighted inside the app.
