# LangCI — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 14, 2026**

LangCI is a free, open-source training and practice app for cochlear implant (CI) users, their families, and audiologists. This policy explains what information LangCI collects, why, and how it is stored.

## 1. Summary

- LangCI runs **entirely on your device**. No account, no cloud, no servers.
- LangCI **never uploads or transmits your data** anywhere.
- LangCI does **not use tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs**.
- You can **delete any data you entered** at any time from within the app.

## 2. What LangCI collects

LangCI stores the following information *locally on your device* to make the app work:

| Category | Examples | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Names | Your name, patient names (in Audiologist mode), family member names | To personalise training and associate sessions with the correct person |
| Audio recordings | Your own voice samples, family voice clips, custom pronunciation recordings | To play back familiar voices during training |
| Training results | Accuracy scores, trial responses, Ling 6 results, fatigue logs, session notes | To show your progress over time |
| Preferences | Selected language, voice accent, active sound list | To remember your settings between launches |

None of this data leaves your device.

## 3. Microphone access

LangCI asks for microphone permission to record voice samples and pronunciation recordings. Recordings are saved only to your device's private app storage. They are never uploaded.

You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings → LangCI → Microphone.

## 4. Third-party patients (Audiologist Mode)

If you use LangCI's Audiologist Mode to record sessions for other patients:

- You are responsible for obtaining the patient's informed consent before entering any of their information.
- The app shows a consent confirmation toggle before each new patient session.
- You can delete a patient and all their associated data at any time from the Patients screen → swipe to delete.

## 5. Children

LangCI is used by cochlear implant users of all ages, including children. LangCI does not knowingly collect personal information from children for any purpose other than to support their CI training. Parents and guardians control what is entered about their children on their device.

## 6. Data sharing

LangCI does **not share your data with anyone**. There is no analytics provider, no crash reporting service, no advertising network. The app has no backend server.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

All data stays on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You can:

- Delete individual recordings, sessions, or patients from within the app.
- Delete all data by uninstalling the app. Deleting the app from your device removes all LangCI data permanently.

## 8. Your rights

Because LangCI stores data only on your device, you are fully in control:

- **Access** — everything LangCI has about you is visible inside the app.
- **Correction** — edit any entry, session, or patient record.
- **Deletion** — remove any item via swipe-to-delete, or remove everything by uninstalling.
- **Portability** — not applicable, as no data is sent to LangCI.

## 9. Medical disclaimer

LangCI is a **training and practice tool**. It is **not a medical device**, does **not diagnose** hearing conditions, and does **not replace clinical evaluation** by a qualified audiologist, speech-language pathologist, or otolaryngologist. Always consult your healthcare provider regarding changes to your hearing or CI programming.

See the **Sources & References** screen in the app for citations to the clinical literature underlying LangCI's training methodology.
